MY EXPERIENCE

2014

Graduated from Beijing 101 Middle School

On behalf of my school's delegation, I visited the United States and Hong Kong (China). I realized my passion for cultural exchange as well as exploring a new environment.

2015

Entered National Chiao Tung University

I chose Information Management and Finance as my major. I believe it is a unique major all over the world, which train students to master knowledge of both technology and finance. I also joined the basketball team of my department and NCTU pop dance club, starting my exciting college life.

2016

Joined NCTU Student Union

I worked at the department of Public Relations first and then became the department head. I organized several big activities with over 10000 participants. Under my lead, the department developed close relations with companies and raised over 200,000 TWD.

2017

Summer Internship at AsiaInfo

As a software enginner, I tested Data Asset Cloud Map and Visual Development Tools and identified over 20 functional issues.

2018

Summer Internship at Deloitte Consulting

I led the team to finish the digital transformation report of gold jewelry retail industry. I learned how to collect and analyze data for a specific industry.

2019

Exchanged at National University of Singapore

I experienced the different Southeast Asia culture and I love it! I made friends with other exchange students from different countries and traveled to Malaysia, Thailand, Vietnam and Cambodia. That's when I started my youtube channel to record my life!

Graduated from NCTU

My GPA was 3.9 / 4.0 and ranked the second at my department. I won the Academic Achievement Awards in NCTU several times. Due to my excellent grade, I was invited to join the Beta Gamma Sigma Honorary Business School Society, which was seen as the dean's list of my college.

Entered Carnegie Mellon University

I was admitted to the Master of Information System Management program and started to advance my technology skills. I learned about OOP, database, distributed system, search engine and NoSQL.

2020

Summer Internship at BizCatcher International
